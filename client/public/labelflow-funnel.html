Priority #3 — Voided Labels

Keep Your Metrics Accurate. Void Labels That Never Shipped.

A voided label is one that was generated but never used — order got cancelled, label was printed by mistake, or the shipment was re-created. Without a void facility, those unused labels pollute your delivery success rate, skew your average transit times, and make tracking reports look broken. Label Flow lets you mark any label voided so it's excluded from every metric that matters.

Priority #7 — ZIP Intelligence

Type a ZIP. City & State Fill Themselves. Wrong ZIP? Caught Before It Ships.

A mismatched ZIP code is the #1 cause of carrier rejections and failed deliveries. Label Flow cross-checks every ZIP against a built-in US database — auto-filling city and state on single labels, and blocking bad rows in bulk uploads before a single label is generated.

Single Label

ZIP auto-fills city & state instantly

Type any valid 5-digit US ZIP code and the city and state fields populate automatically — no copy-pasting, no lookups. Works on both the FROM and TO address.
