FAQ

Straight answers

How are rates lower than retail?

We aggregate volume across thousands of sellers to access commercial-style pricing and surface it in one quote — you bring the orders, we bring the leverage.

Which marketplaces sync?

Amazon, Walmart, eBay, Shopify, Etsy, TikTok Shop, Mercari, and more on the roadmap. Growth+ unlocks unlimited integrations.

Do you support USPS manifests?

Yes — generate end-of-day manifests for eligible batches so drop-offs scan clean at the dock.

Refunds & voids?

Carrier rules apply; void windows and refund timing follow USPS/FedEx/UPS policies. In-app status keeps you from guessing.

Can I switch from another tool?

Absolutely — import CSVs day one while you connect stores. Most teams run parallel for a week, then cut over.

What support do I get?

Starter includes email. Growth adds priority channels. Enterprise gets named coverage and SLAs.

Is my data secure?

TLS everywhere, encrypted credentials, least-privilege access for staff roles, and audit-friendly exports.

Cancel anytime?

Yes — monthly plans cancel on your renewal date. Yearly plans are prorated per policy shown at checkout.
